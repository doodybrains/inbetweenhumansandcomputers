In Between Humans and Computers is a research practice by emma rae bruml. It is a study, in other words, of the computer mouse.

The practice takes the form of writing, performance lectures, teaching, and the co-organization of an biennial conference. The practice, in all its forms, is ongoing:

  • A Scrolling Lecture
  • Close To The Metal, for Real Life Magazine
  • Anti-Capitalist Mouse
  • Computer Mouse Conference 2019
  • If You Give A Mouse Gilbert Simondon (forthcoming)
  • Complication of the Computer Mouse, for Queer.Archive.Work
  • The Mouse Holds Us
  • Complication of the Computer Mouse (Annotated), for future feed
  • Computer Mouse Conference 2021
  • The Dada of All Demos
  • Computer Mouse Conference 2023 (details tba)
