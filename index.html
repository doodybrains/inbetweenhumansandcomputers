In Between Humans and Computers is a research practice by emma rae bruml. It is a study, in other words, of the computer mouse. The practice takes the form of writing, performance lectures, teaching, and the co-organization of The Computer Mouse Conference with Ashley Jane Lewis. The practice, in all its forms, is ongoing.
